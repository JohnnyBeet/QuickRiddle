Text Riddles

Those riddles are in form of a sentence that describes an object or idea that the user needs to guess. Your answer should consist of one word or letter.

For example: What's in the middle of nowhere? Answer: H

About Quick Riddle

Quick Riddle is an app that selects random quiz from the riddle database and checks your answer to that riddle. It also saves Your personal statistics. There are three difficulty levels that You can pick from! Don't worry! All answers are case insensitive.

Math Riddles

Those riddles are in polish. They are quizes from international mathematics competition called Kangur Matematyczny. Answers are provided with riddle content and are represented by letters. You answer is one of those letters.

Jeżeli 17x + 51y = 102, to 9x + 27y = A) 54 B) 36. C) 34 D) 18 E) 12. Answer: A
